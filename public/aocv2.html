Antibody-Oligonucleotide Conjugate (AOC™) Platform

1. TfR1 Targeting

Anti-TfR1 mAb binds transferrin receptor 1 on muscle cells with high specificity

2. Endocytosis

Receptor-mediated internalization delivers AOC-1044 into muscle cytoplasm

3. PMO Release

Linker cleavage releases PMO payload for nuclear translocation and pre-mRNA binding

4. Exon Skipping

PMO binds exon 44 splice sites, causing exclusion during mRNA processing

Anti-TfR1 Antibody

  • • Humanized monoclonal antibody
  • • Fc effector function null (no immune activation)
  • • Does not interfere with transferrin binding
  • • Half-life: ~2-3 weeks
Platform Details

Proprietary Linker
