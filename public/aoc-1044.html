AOC-1044 utilizes Avidity's proprietary Antibody Oligonucleotide Conjugate (AOC) platform, combining:

  • Monoclonal antibody targeting transferrin receptor 1 (TfR1) for muscle-specific delivery
  • Phosphorodiamidate morpholino oligomer (PMO) designed to skip exon 44
  • Proprietary linker optimizing drug delivery and stability

This innovative approach achieves 50-fold greater PMO concentrations in skeletal muscle compared to unconjugated PMOs, enabling effective exon skipping and dystrophin restoration.

Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy Background

Disease Overview

Duchenne muscular dystrophy (DMD) is a severe, progressive neuromuscular disorder caused by mutations in the dystrophin gene. It is characterized by:

  • Progressive muscle weakness and degeneration
  • Loss of ambulation typically by age 12
  • Respiratory and cardiac complications
  • Life expectancy typically in the 20s-30s

Epidemiology

Global Prevalence: 7.1 per 100,000 males
Birth Prevalence: 19.8 per 100,000 live male births
General Population: 2.8 per 100,000
