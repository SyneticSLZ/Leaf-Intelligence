Traditional Approach (Mixed Population)

Example: Atomoxetine Trial
• Total enrolled: 2,977 patients
• Biomarker-positive (responders): 208 (7%)
• Biomarker-negative: 2,769 (93%)
• Timeline: 48+ months
• Estimated cost: $250M+
Effect dilution: True effect in biomarker-positive patients diluted by including 93% non-responders, requiring massive sample size.

Enriched Approach (Liberal Divisions)

Example: Nusinersen Trial
• Total enrolled: 121 patients
• Biomarker-positive: 121 (100%)
• Biomarker-negative: 0 (0%)
• Timeline: 24 months
• Estimated cost: $85M
Optimal power: Pure effect size measurement in target population, dramatically reduced sample size needed.
